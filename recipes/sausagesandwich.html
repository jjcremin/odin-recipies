Sausage Sandwich

Ingredients

  • sausages
  • Butter
  • Bread
  • Ketchup(optional)

Steps:

  1. Place the sausages into a hot well oiled pan
  2. Optional: add the bread to a toaster
  3. Turning every minute of so, cook the sausages until golden brown
  4. Butter the bread or toast
  5. Add the sausages to a slice of bread then top with the remaining slice
  6. Optional: Squirt on some ketchup
  7. Enjoy!
