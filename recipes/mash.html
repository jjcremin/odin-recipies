Mashed Potatoes

Beatuifully creamy mashed potatoes finsihed with a sprinkle of chive.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Water(for boiling)
  • Milk/Cream
  • Salt & Pepper (to taste)

Steps:

  1. Start by peeling the potatoes, while doing this its best to begin to boil the water in a large pot
  2. Season the water in the pot, then add the potatoes
  3. Allow the potatoes to cook until they can be easily pierced with a fork
  4. Drain the potatoes, and add back to the pot on very low heat
  5. Begin to mash the potatoes, until fairly mashed
  6. Add the butter and mash again, before adding the milk/cream to get desired texture
  7. Season to taste with salt and pepper
