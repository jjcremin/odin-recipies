Bacon Sandwhich

Amzing Sandwich with crispy bacon

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Butter
  • Bread
  • Ketchup(optional)

Steps:

  1. Place the bacon into a hot well oiled pan
  2. Optional: add the bread to a toaster
  3. Turning every minute of so, cook the bacon until golden brown and crispy
  4. Butter the bread or toast
  5. Add the bacon to a slice of bread then top with the remaining slice
  6. Optional: Squirt on some ketchup
  7. Enjoy!
